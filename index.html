About Me

Hi! I'm Davide Righetti, a digital marketing analyst holding a Master’s degree in Management Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, with a specialization in Digital Business and Market Innovation.

After an initial professional experience as a Technology Consulting Analyst at Accenture S.p.A., one of the world’s leading consulting firms, I decided to fully apply both my corporate and academic background in a real-world, high-impact environment.

Over the years, I have worked across multiple areas of digital and marketing operations, including data analysis and cloud infrastructure (AWS Certified), copywriting and communication, social media strategy, Meta advertising, affiliate programs, talent campaigns, and content creation. Alongside execution, my work has consistently involved performance KPI definition, market and competitive analysis, design thinking, and offer positioning.

Having studied — and later applied professionally — digital business models at a time when these disciplines were still emerging, allowed me to build practical experience early, translating theory into execution and delivering measurable results in both marketing performance and strategic analysis.
